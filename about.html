About

Engineering rigor, business results

Built on decades of experience solving hard problems in high-stakes environments.

Peak Consults was founded on a simple observation: most businesses are overpaying for technology, under-utilizing what they have, and missing massive opportunities to automate.

With 25 years of software engineering experience — primarily in the nuclear energy sector, where precision, reliability, and safety aren't optional — we bring a level of engineering discipline that's rare in business consulting.

We've seen how the right technology decisions compound over time. A well-architected system saves money every single day. An automated process frees people for work that actually matters. Eliminating one unnecessary SaaS subscription can save tens of thousands per year.

Our approach is simple: understand your business deeply, identify the highest-impact opportunities, and implement solutions that deliver measurable results. No frameworks. No buzzwords. Just practical engineering applied to real business problems.
