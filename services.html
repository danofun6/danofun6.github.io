Hands-on consulting that delivers.

Four practices designed to cut costs, eliminate inefficiency, and position your business for sustainable growth — with measurable targets agreed up front.

01 — Optimize

Operations & SaaS rationalization

Most businesses are paying for tools they don’t need, running processes that should be automated, and maintaining systems that create more work than they save. We fix that.

We audit your technology stack, workflows, and operational processes to identify waste, redundancy, and opportunity — with clear dollar targets and a roadmap.

  • Technology stack audit and rationalization
  • SaaS subscription optimization and consolidation
  • Workflow analysis and streamlining
  • Process documentation and standardization
  • Vendor evaluation and contract negotiation support
  • Cost reduction roadmap with measurable targets

02 — Automate

Automation & AI implementation

Right-sized automation doesn’t just save time — it removes whole categories of work. We identify the highest-impact opportunities, prove value, and only then scale.

From workflow automation to AI-powered systems, we build solutions matched to your actual needs — not hype-driven technology for its own sake.

  • Business process automation
  • AI strategy and implementation planning
  • Custom AI tooling and integration
  • Data pipeline automation
  • Reporting and analytics automation
  • Integration between existing systems

Software architecture & review

Bad architecture decisions compound over time. Whether you’re building something new or inheriting a legacy system, the right foundation saves enormous cost down the road.

With decades of experience building mission-critical software in regulated, high-stakes industries, we bring engineering rigor to every engagement.

  • System architecture design and review
  • Legacy system modernization planning
  • Build-vs-buy analysis
  • Technical debt assessment and reduction
  • Security architecture review
  • Scalability and performance planning

Technology strategy

Technology decisions should be driven by business outcomes, not trends. We help you build a roadmap that aligns every dollar spent with measurable business value.

  • Executive technology advisory
